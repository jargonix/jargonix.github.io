Open MSG · EML Viewer & Reader - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to the Open MSG · EML Viewer & Reader application. Privacy and the security of your data are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information and files.

  1. 100% Offline Processing (Zero Server Uploads) Our core functionality—parsing, reading, searching, and exporting your .msg and .eml files—is performed entirely locally on your device (iPhone, iPad, or Mac). We do not upload, transmit, store, or have any access to your email content, attachments, metadata, or contacts. Your files stay securely on your device at all times.

  2. Information We Collect Because the app works offline, our data collection is strictly limited to necessary third-party integrations:

RevenueCat (In-App Purchases): We use RevenueCat to process and manage subscription plans. RevenueCat may collect an anonymous user ID and store your purchase history to ensure your Premium status is synced across devices. No personal identifying information (like your real name or contact email) is accessible to us.

Google AdMob (Advertising): For free users, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect basic interaction data and non-identifiable device identifiers to serve ads. Premium users are entirely exempt from any advertising tracking.

Crash Analytics: We may collect anonymized crash reports (e.g., via Apple's native diagnostic tools) purely to identify bugs and improve the app. This does not contain any content from the files you open.

  1. Data Retention and Deletion Since we do not store your data on our servers, there is no user data or email history for us to retain or delete. You have complete control over your files on your own device.

  2. Third-Party Links Our application may contain links to external sites (e.g., this privacy policy page). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites.

  3. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

  4. Contact Us If you have any questions or suggestions send message: /contact/
