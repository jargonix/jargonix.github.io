DjVu Reader ("we," "our," or "us") respects privacy. Our core functionality (reading, rendering, and managing your `.djvu` and `.djv` files) works completely offline on your device. We do not upload, read, or distribute your personal documents to any remote servers. When you use the "WiFi Transfer" feature, files are transferred locally over your own secure network.

To improve our app's performance, we may collect anonymous crash reports and basic usage statistics (such as which features are most frequently used). This data contains no personally identifiable information (PII) and no content from your files.
