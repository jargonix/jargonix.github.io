  1. On-Device Processing CRT Viewer is designed with a strict on-device processing architecture:

Certificate Parsing: All X.509 certificate parsing (PEM, DER, CRT, CER, PFX/P12) is performed locally using in the App. Format Conversion: Certificate format conversions (PEM ↔ CRT ↔ CER ↔ DER) occur entirely on your device. PDF Generation: Certificate reports are generated locally and never touch any server. File Access: The App only accesses files you explicitly select through the iOS file picker. It does not scan, index, or access any other files on your device.

  1. Data Storage The App stores the following data locally on your device:

App Preferences: Language selection, theme settings, onboarding completion status. Subscription Status: A local cache of your Pro subscription status for offline access. Encrypted Certificate Vault: If you choose to save certificates to the Vault, they are stored locally on your device in an encrypted format. This data never leaves your device. Domain Watch List: Domains you add to the SSL Monitor are stored locally for notification scheduling. CSR/Key Pairs: Generated CSRs and private keys exist only in memory during your session. They are not stored persistently unless you explicitly download them.

Certificate data viewed in the main viewer exists only in memory during your active session and is discarded when you close the certificate or exit the App.
