Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

At PCAP File Viewer · Reader, privacy is our top priority. We have built this application with a "privacy-first" and "offline-first" approach. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data.

Our core philosophy is simple: Your network data belongs to you, and it stays on your device.

  1. NO COLLECTION OF NETWORK DATA OR PCAP FILES The App functions entirely as a local analysis tool.

Local Parsing: All PCAP, PCAPNG, and CAP files you open are parsed and analyzed locally on your device's hardware.

No Uploads: We DO NOT upload, transmit, read, or store the contents of your packet captures on any of our servers.

Offline Functionality: The App is designed to work completely offline, ensuring that your sensitive network infrastructure data never leaves your secure environment.

  1. DATA WE PROCESS We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use the App. To manage subscriptions and premium features, we process a minimal amount of anonymous data:

Subscription and Purchase Data (via RevenueCat) If you choose to subscribe to a PRO plan or purchase Lifetime Access, we use a third-party service called RevenueCat to manage and validate your subscription status securely.

Data Involved: Anonymous Apple App Store Receipt Data, transaction ID, purchase date, and subscription status.

Purpose: To unlock PRO features across your devices and restore previous purchases.

Association: This data is linked to an anonymous, randomly generated user ID. We cannot link this data to your personal identity.

  1. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES & NO TRACKING We keep third-party involvement to an absolute minimum to protect your privacy:

RevenueCat: Used solely for processing In-App Purchases and Subscriptions.

Apple App Store: Handles the actual payment processing securely.

NO ADVERTISEMENTS: The App contains NO advertising SDKs (such as AdMob or others). We provide a completely ad-free experience.

NO ANALYTICS / TRACKING: The App contains NO tracking or analytics frameworks. We do not track your usage behavior, the buttons you tap, or how long you use the App.

  1. DATA SECURITY Because your network data remains local, its security relies on your iOS device's security.

  2. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time if we introduce new features that require explanations. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

  3. CONTACT US If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at: Support & Contact URL: /contact/
