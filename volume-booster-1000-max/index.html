Chrome Extension: Download / İndir

  1. No Personal Data Collection We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII). The extension functions entirely within your local browser environment. We have no access to your browsing history, identity, emails, or passwords.

  2. Local Data Usage The extension uses the chrome.storage.local API exclusively to save your session preferences. This data remains on your physical machine and is never uploaded to any external servers. This includes: ➖ Custom Volume Levels: Preferred gain levels for specific websites. ➖ Audio Profiles: Selected modes (Normal, Bass Boost, Vocal Crystal). ➖ User Interface Settings: Your preference for Dark or Light mode.

  3. Permissions Justification (Manifest V3 Compliance) To provide real-time audio amplification while adhering to the latest security models, we use the following permissions: ➖ tabCapture & offscreen: Used to securely capture the audio stream of the active tab for processing. Offscreen documents are used to handle high-quality audio processing in a separate, isolated environment for better performance. ➖ activeTab & tabs: Used to identify the current website context and apply your saved volume settings automatically. ➖ storage: Used to remember your site-specific preferences across different sessions.

  4. "AI-Safe" Technology Our "AI-Safe" audio processing is executed 100% client-side. It analyzes audio signals in real-time to prevent speaker distortion or hardware damage. No audio data is ever recorded, sampled, or sent to any cloud processing units.

  5. Technical Links The extension may contain links to our official website (hesaplama.vip). Once you leave the extension's interface, please refer to the website’s (hesaplama.vip and plusapkz.com) own privacy policy for its data handling practices.
